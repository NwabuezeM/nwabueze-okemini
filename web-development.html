What is Frontend Development?

Frontend Development, often referred to as "client-side" development, is the process of building the visible part of a website or web application that users interact with directly. It involves creating the user interface (UI) and implementing the design to ensure a seamless and engaging user experience.

In simpler terms, if your website were a house, the frontend would be the exterior, the doors, the windows â€“ everything that visitors see and interact with. It's about making sure that your online space not only looks appealing but also functions intuitively, keeping your audience hooked and satisfied.
