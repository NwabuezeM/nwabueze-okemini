FRONTEND WEB DEVELOPER AND WORDPRESS DEVELOPER

I'm Nwabueze Okemini Wisdom, a versatile frontend web developer and WordPress specialist, dedicated to creating dynamic and visually captivating websites that seamlessly blend creativity and functionality.

BACKGROUND

I am from Nigeria and hold a Higher National Diploma (with Distinction) in Electrical/Electronics Engineering with a specialization in Electronics and Telecommunication. During my academic years, I undertook industrial training at the Federal Radio Corporation of Nigeria (FRCN) Unity FM in Abakaliki, Ebonyi State.

Following my academic pursuits, I fulfilled my compulsory National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) in Cross River State, where I taught secondary school mathematics. Driven by a childhood aspiration to become a software engineer, I relocated to Port Harcourt, Rivers State, initiating a self-taught journey in web development.

I have successfully evolved into a proficient frontend developer, emphasizing coding expertise. In addition, I specialize in creating websites using WordPress and developing ecommerce websites with WooCommerce. Currently, I am actively taking courses in backend development with the goal of becoming a MERN Stack developer. Additionally, I am expanding my skill set by learning other programming languages such as Python and Dart, and I aspire to delve into mobile app development. This showcases my dedication to continuous learning and professional growth in the dynamic field of technology.
